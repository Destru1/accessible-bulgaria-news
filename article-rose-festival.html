The annual Rose Festival in Kazanlak has drawn thousands of visitors from around the world. The event celebrates Bulgaria's centuries-old tradition of rose oil production and features parades, music, and local crafts.

Visitors can enjoy rose-picking rituals, folk performances, and taste local delicacies. The festival is a major highlight in Bulgaria's cultural calendar and supports the local economy by promoting tourism and traditional crafts.

The Rose Valley is renowned for producing some of the world's finest rose oil, a key ingredient in many luxury perfumes.
