The Rila Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage site, marks its 1,100th anniversary with special exhibitions and religious ceremonies. The monastery remains a symbol of Bulgarian culture and spirituality.

Founded in the 10th century, Rila Monastery is renowned for its stunning architecture, vibrant frescoes, and historical significance. It attracts pilgrims and tourists from all over the world.

Anniversary events include art exhibitions, concerts, and guided tours highlighting the monastery's enduring legacy.
