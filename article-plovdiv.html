The ancient Roman theater in Plovdiv has reopened after extensive restoration. The site, one of the best-preserved in Europe, will host concerts and cultural events throughout the year.

Plovdiv, one of the oldest cities in the world, is known for its rich history and vibrant cultural scene. The restoration project has brought new life to the theater, making it a must-visit attraction for tourists and locals alike.

Upcoming events include music festivals, theatrical performances, and international gatherings celebrating Plovdiv's heritage.
